About Us




Our mission


Our mission is to protect animals, relieve their suffering, advance their health and welfare, prevent cruelty, and work for a just and compassionate society.



Our Work


We are a national and international leader in animal protection and veterinary medicine and provide direct hands-on care for thousands of animals each year. Founded in 2016, we are one of the newer animal shelters in the United States, but we have quickly grown and hope you can help us continue to grow. We aim to improve the lives of animals through hands-on work as well as through our education program.


We are currently a team of 8 veterinary professionals, 32 animal care professionals, and over hundreds of part time volunteers. We all work together to provide the best care we can to as many animals in need that we can.
