About Us


Our mission


Our mission is to protect animals, relieve their suffering, advance their health and welfare, prevent cruelty, and work for a just and compassionate society.



Our Work


We are a national and international leader in animal protection and veterinary medicine and provide direct hands-on care for thousands of animals each year. Founded in 1868, we are one of the oldest animal shelters in the United States. We aim to improve the lives of animals through hands-on work as well as through our education program.
