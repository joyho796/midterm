Store




Bring home a treat


100% of the profits from our store go towards helping the lives of animals in need. Buying something from our store is a great and easy way to help out while still bringing something home for yourself or your pet.


IMPORTANT NOTICE:
Due to the pandemic, we strongly encourage you to make your orders through our online store.




Mystery Box Event


We are currently selling items in a mystery box style! This means you choose what kind of items you want to bring home, and we'll randomly select some surprise items for you.

Simply specify who you are buying the items for (your dog, your cat, your turtle, yourself, etc.) and we will pick out appropriate gifts for you.



Meal Box ($7 each): 

Meal Boxes can contain kibble, canned foods, biscuits, and various treats for you or your pets. The boxes will be picked based on the recipient you indicate.


Toy ($5 each): 

Toys are also customized and picked based on the recipient you indicate. We have a very large selection of toys and hope you will enjoy your gift.


Care Coupon ($11 each): 

Care Coupons can be used for your pets' veterinary care at our animal care clinic. They apply to any pet you may own.


Merch Item ($15 each): 

We have various merch items for humans as well as pets! Inlcuding but not limited to hats, shirts, pens and scarves.
